Our Facilities

Our campus boasts a wide range of modern facilities designed to enhance your learning experience and support your personal growth. From cutting-edge technology to recreational spaces, we provide everything you need to thrive academically and socially. Explore some of our key facilities below:

Modern Library

Our state-of-the-art library offers a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support your academic journey.

Advanced Computer Labs

Our computer labs are equipped with the latest hardware and software to provide hands-on experience in various technologies.

Sports Complex

Our multi-purpose sports complex caters to various indoor and outdoor sports, promoting physical fitness and team spirit.

Bus Facility

Our college provides convenient and reliable bus services for students and staff, ensuring safe and timely transportation to and from campus.

Hostel Facility

Our college provides comfortable and secure hostel facilities for students, ensuring a conducive living environment.

Student Cafeteria

Our modern cafeteria offers a variety of nutritious and delicious meals, providing a great space for students to relax and socialize.

About the College

RRSIMT is a premier engineering institute located in Amethi, Uttar Pradesh. Established with a vision to provide quality technical education, our college offers a wide range of courses in various engineering disciplines. With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a focus on practical learning, RRSIMT prepares students for successful careers in the ever-evolving field of technology.
