Computer Science & Engineering

Overview

The B.Tech Programme in computer science and Engineering is for students seeking a broad and deep knowledge of hardware, software and theoretical aspects of high speed computing devices and with the application of these devices to scientific technological & business problems.
This course is designed to Equip the Students for Professional Career within the Computer industry, particularly those who wish to gain Employment in International IT Companies & prepares Students for Post Graduate study.
Computer Science & Engineering have become interdependent on each other. The reason being the impact of computerization and its versatile capabilities to enhance productivity, efficiency, speed, accuracy & economy in work culture.
